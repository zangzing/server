Your Homepage

Your homepage includes three views: Album Grid View (shows My Albums, Liked and Followed Albums, and Invited Albums), People View, and Activity View. We've added a few of our albums to get you started.

To get back to your homepage, just click the ZangZing logo in the top left corner.

Create a Group

Every ZangZing album is a group album. One or many people can add photos.

In the Create Group & Share step of a new album, simply let Everyone add photos or Create a Group and invite friends as Contributors or Viewers.
