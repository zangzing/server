Welcome to ZangZing

Thanks for joining ZangZing, a new way for groups to share photos simply and beautifully.

We're still in Beta. This means that there are likely to be some minor issues. If you find some, please let us know by using the Help button in the top right corner.

Click Next and we will walk you through 5 key steps about how to use ZangZing.

Your ZangZing Home Page

Your home page includes the albums you create and the albums you Like. And, when you Like a person, you will see his or her Public albums. We've added a few of our albums to get you started.

Whenever you want to get back to your homepage, just click the ZangZing logo in the top left corner.

Create Your First Album

To create a group album, click the Create button on the left side of the bottom tool bar.

Then follow the steps. You can skip steps or go to previous steps too.

Privacy Matters

The best part of creating an album is sharing your memories with the right people.

Each album can be either Public, Hidden, or Password Protected.

Invite Contributors

Every ZangZing album is a group album.
One or many people can add photos.

In the Contributors step of creating your album, simply invite friends and they can add photos through email, their computer, or various photo sites.
